02

ProtMerge

A Python desktop application that accepts a list of UniProt IDs or gene names and aggregates data from UniProt, ProtParam, the Human Protein Atlas, PDB, and optionally NCBI BLAST. Outputs multi-sheet Excel workbooks with clickable hyperlinks, amino-acid composition and physicochemical properties (MW, pI, GRAVY, extinction coefficients), environment detection, and BLAST similarity hits. Built around a Tkinter GUI with file/column selection, real-time progress tracking, and automatic rate-limit handling for NCBI services.

03

Polymerization Kinetics

An original laboratory experiment I designed from scratch to investigate ethyl cyanoacrylate polymerization kinetics by UV-Vis and fluorescence spectroscopy. Unlike a standard lab with a known answer, this work proposes a new protocol to extract pseudo-first-order rate constants and probe the Trommsdorff-Norrish gel effect, connecting kinetic behavior to polymer structure and polydispersity. The emphasis is on the experimental design itself, building a reproducible procedure that future students or researchers could actually run.
